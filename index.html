Why was our air so polluted? What effects did this have?

Most air pollution is caused by human lifestyle factors, mainly from usage of factories, automobiles, cigarettes, and planes.

How the air healed as we got sick

Several major cities are seeing unprecedented drastic decreases in air pollution due to partially shutting down their economies. This mainly came from decreased factory output and automobile use.


In India, the Himalayas are visible for the first time in decades. In Los Angeles, our rush hour traffic finally disappeared and our skyline is finally clearly visible. In Nepal, the mountains are clearer than ever.
